Everyday Questions

Play a game of Everyday Questions which plays in a similar format to Family Fortunes/Feud.

The player is given an everyday question such as "Name a country that speaks Spanish". The players aim is to guess the top 10 answers provided by the public. The more common the answer the higher score the player will achieve for guessing it.

Where Are We?

Where are we, is a geography based game including a variety of questions including "Guess the flag, Guess the country and Guess the capital".

The player is given a variety of geography questions ranging from identifying a country by its flag or its outline. Or alteratively, guess the capital of a country or vice-versa.

Name 3 things

Can you name 3 things in the given time limit?

The player is given a question/topic and has to provide 3 answers within 5 seconds.

Spelling Bee

How good is your spelling? Find out by playing spelling bee.

The player has to spell the word that they hear in the given time limit. Each round will provide a more difficult word until the player fails.
